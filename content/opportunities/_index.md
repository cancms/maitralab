## <div style="text-align: center;">Opportunities</div> 
## &nbsp;

We look for highly motivated, team-oriented and hard-working candidates who seek to enjoy the excitement of the scientific journey and welcome the challenge of a high pace, energitic environment.

Graduate Students can apply to the NYU Vilcek Institute of Graduate Biomedical Sciences. Once admitted to the NYU Vilcek graduate program, graduate students interested in joining the Hernando Lab could rotate in the lab if positions are available.

If interested, please email Dr. Hernando with your scientific interests and a copy of your resume.
